## 📖 What is manga4life?

manga4life is a fan-made reading platform born out of frustration with the modern web — cluttered sites, aggressive ads, and layouts that fight for your attention instead of respecting it. This project strips all of that away and puts the story front and center.

> *"Most reading platforms are built to keep you clicking. manga4life is built to keep you reading. There's a difference — and it shows on every page."*

It was created with one simple goal in mind:

**make reading simple again.**

---

## ✨ Core Features

Designed around what actually matters to a reader.

| Feature | Description |
|---|---|
| 🖼 **Distraction-free reader** | Full-width panels, no sidebars, no pop-ups. Just the art and the story. |
| ⚡ **Lightweight & fast** | Minimal JS, zero trackers, loads instantly even on slow connections. |
| 🚫 **Completely ad-free** | No banners, no autoplay, no cookie walls. Ever. |
| 🌙 **Dark mode support** | Easy on the eyes for late-night reading sessions. |
| 📱 **Mobile-friendly** | Swipe-based navigation optimized for reading on any device. |
| 🔖 **Reading progress** | Picks up right where you left off, chapter by chapter. |

---

## 🎯 Reading Experience

Every detail is built around comfort and focus.

- **Content-first layout** — Panels fill the full viewport. Navigation is minimal and hidden until needed.
- **Smooth chapter navigation** — Jump between chapters without leaving the page or losing your place.
- **Adjustable reading direction** — Switch between left-to-right (manhwa / webtoon) and right-to-left (manga) modes.
- **Keyboard & gesture support** — Arrow keys on desktop, swipe gestures on mobile. Reading feels natural.
- **No account required** — Open the site, find a title, start reading. No sign-up, no wall.

## 💬 Contributing & Feedback

manga4life is a community project and every piece of feedback helps shape it into something better.

**Found a bug?**
Open an issue and describe what happened. Screenshots always help.

**Have a feature idea?**
If something would make reading better, share it. No idea is too small.

**Want to contribute code?**
Pull requests are reviewed and merged if they align with the project's direction. Keep it clean, keep it reader-first.

manga4life is still growing. Your feedback directly influences what gets built next.
